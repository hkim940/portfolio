    Overview

    FutureScape™ is a modeling and simulation platform that creates digital replicas of large systems, such as entire cities or large industrial infrastructure. Inside the digital twin, FutureScape puts users in control to run scenarios and experiment with solutions. 
    - futurescape.ai

    FutureScape is a modeling and simulation platform where users can run simulations to test their what if? solutions. Thus allowing users to understand and analyze the impact of future event that may take place.

    As one of the developer within Dashboard Development Team. My role was to design and develop a dashboard where users can

    • Configure Detail of the Simulation
    • Launch Simulation and track progress of Simulation
    • Display results of the simulation in Analyics Web App

    Tech Stack

    In order to generate the dashboard, several different technologies were used. Angular JS, Node.js, AWS, Plotly.js, D3 and more.
